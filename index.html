01 — About

A systems person who likes things to just work.

I'm an IT systems & DevOps generalist who's happiest across the whole stack — from the OS and the network up to the services and the code running on top.

I was always pulled toward IT. While studying Wood Science at Stellenbosch, the computers, networks and servers were the part I couldn't put down — so I moved into infrastructure full time rather than finish the degree.

At Dijiti Solutions I came in as the first dedicated ops hire and built the company's IT, cloud and networking stack from the ground up — then ran it day to day for a 25-person team. I like tracing a problem across layers and joining up systems that need to talk to each other, whether that's AWS, a Proxmox host, a database or a misbehaving DNS resolver.

I'm recently off the back of my time at Dijiti and looking for my next systems, infrastructure or networking role — with the range to take on development work alongside it.
